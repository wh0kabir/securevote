FAQs

How does SecureVote prevent duplicate voting?

Each voter's wallet address is cryptographically signed and recorded on-chain. Our smart contracts enforce one vote per verified identity, detected in real-time.

Can votes be traced back to me?

No. We use zero-knowledge proofs (zk-SNARKs) to validate eligibility without exposing personal data. Your wallet address is never linked to your vote choice.

What happens if I lose my wallet credentials?

During KYC, we provide a recovery seed phrase encrypted with your government ID. This allows secure vote retrieval without compromising anonymity.

How are election results verified?

Results are computed on-chain via smart contracts and can be independently audited using our open-source verifier tool. Every step is transparent.

Which blockchains do you support?

Currently Ethereum and Polygon for low fees. Coming soon: Solana, Avalanche, and our own L2 chain optimized for voting.
